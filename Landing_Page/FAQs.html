Frequently Asked Questions


Is FlexPulse suitable for all fitness levels?

Yes! FlexPulse caters to users of all fitness levels, from beginners to advanced. The app offers a variety of workouts and allows you to customize your fitness plan based on your abilities and goals.


Can I use the app without a gym membership or equipment?

Absolutely! FlexPulse provides workouts that can be done at home or anywhere without the need for specialized equipment. Whether you prefer bodyweight exercises or have minimal equipment, we have options for you.


Is my personal information safe?

We take privacy and data security seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Is there a free trial period for the premium version?

Yes, we offer a free trial period for the premium version of FlexPulse. During the trial, you'll have access to all premium features. After the trial ends, you can choose to subscribe to our premium plan for continued access.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can manage and cancel your subscription directly through your app store account settings. Please refer to the app store's instructions for details on canceling subscriptions.
